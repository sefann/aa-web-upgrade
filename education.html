COACHING PROGRAM

AA Tax Lab

Master Tax Strategy & Build Financial Confidence

AA Tax Lab is a structured, 6-week intensive training program designed to equip participants with both the foundational knowledge of tax preparation and the practical skills to confidently use UltraTax software. Developed and hosted by Nikki Bushaw, CPA/CTC, the Lab provides a hands-on learning environment where theory meets practice, ensuring every participant leaves with the ability to prepare tax returns accurately and consistently.

What Participants Will Learn

Tax Prep 101 Fundamentals

  • Filing statuses, dependents, income types, deductions, and credits
  • Identifying and organizing required tax documents (W-2s, 1099s, mortgage interest statements, etc.)
  • Recognizing common taxpayer situations and compliance requirements
  • Understand the purpose and scope of tax preparation
  • Tax lifecycle overview: collection → entry → review → filing
  • Compliance vs. advisory services
  • Setting expectations around timelines and deliverables
  • "Audit ready" documentation for review and peace of mind

UltraTax Software Mastery

  • Navigation and data entry workflows
  • Document import, diagnostics, and error-checking
  • Preparing, reviewing, and finalizing returns in UltraTax
  • Live demonstrations of UltraTax navigation and workflows
  • Step-by-step instruction on entering W-2s, 1099s, credits, and deductions
  • Diagnostics and error-checking - guided use of diagnostics to identify and correct errors
  • Real client scenarios demonstrated in UltraTax
  • Best practices for ease and success

Real-World Case Studies & Quality Control

  • Practical application using anonymized client data
  • Group exercises and problem-solving activities
  • End-to-end preparation of a full return as a capstone project
  • Using UltraTax diagnostic tools to spot and correct errors
  • Internal best practices for review and finalization
  • Building consistency across preparers and teams
