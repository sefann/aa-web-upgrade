Speed & Efficiency

Faster Turnaround Times

Our process improvements mean you get your returns completed faster than ever:

  • Individual returns: 3-5 business days (complete documents)
  • Business returns: 5-10 business days (complete documents)
  • Automated processing for straightforward returns
  • Priority processing available for urgent needs
  • Real-time progress tracking in TaxDome

We make every effort to fulfill these turnaround times. However, because the process is heavily dependent on the information provided by the client, these timeframes can only be met if all required documentation is submitted in full and all questions are answered promptly. If these conditions are not satisfied, we cannot commit to the stated turnaround times.
